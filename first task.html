HEGEL

Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel 27 August 1770 14 November 1831 was a German philosopher and one of the most influential figures of German idealism and 19th-century

philosophy. His influence extends across the entire range of contemporary philosophical topics, from metaphysical issues in epistemology and ontology, to political philosophy,

the philosophy of history, philosophy of art, philosophy of religion, and the history of philosophy.
